Shipping Information

For orders over R599, we deliver free of charge within South Africa, either door-to-door or to a pick-up point, depending on the suburb and postal code selected at checkout.

For orders under R599, we charge a non-refundable R65 fee to deliver either door-to-door or delivery to a point, depending on where you’re located.

During the checkout process, we will determine your delivery options based on your delivery suburb and postal code. Irrespective of the delivery method selected, we will deliver within 2 – 6 working days depending on the delivery address.

Delivery fees and proposed delivery times are summarised below:

Area Order Value Delivery Type Delivery Time Delivery Fee
Main centres in SA Over R599 Door-to-door 3 – 7 working days FREE
Outlying areas in SA Over R599 Door-to-door 5 – 9 working days R200
Main centres in SA Under R599 Door-to-door 3 – 7 working days R69
Outlying areas in SA Under R599 Door-to-door 5 – 9 working days R200

For more information on shipping, visit Aramax.

Collection is available from our Beacon Bay branch and or Vincent branch. Collection is free of charge no matter the value. Purchases can usually be collected within 2 - 5 working days after payment has been made.
